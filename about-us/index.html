About Us

Our osteopaths may play a significant role in pain management or when used in conjunction with medical treatment. Where required we work in consultation with GPs, specialists, naturopaths, TCM practitioners and podiatrists towards bettering your health. You do not need a referral from a GP to see an osteopath at Embody Healthcare.

At Embody Healthcare your Osteopath will develop a treatment plan tailored to your individual needs, based on your condition and lifestyle factors. Treatment by your osteopath usually consists of a variety of techniques which include, but is not limited to, massage, stretching, joint manipulation and/or mobilisation.

We may also advise you on appropriate home- or gym-based strengthening and stretching exercises, effective posture, and home and workplace ergonomics.

All the Osteopaths at Embody Healthcare have completed a five-year University degree, and are committed to furthering their knowledge base by undertaking a minimum of 25 hours of Continuing Professional Development studies per year.

For more information about Osteopathy, please refer to the Osteopathy Australia website
